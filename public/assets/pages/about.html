LOCATION
MIND Milano Innovation District

TRAIN
The main entrance to the concert area (ACCESS TRIULZA) is located near the RHO-FIERA station, reachable by the following trains:
  • SUBURBAN LINES
    • S5 (VARESE – MILANO – TREVIGLIO)
    • S6 (NOVARA – MILANO – TREVIGLIO)
    • S11 (CHIASSO – COMO – MILANO)
  • REGIONAL LINES
    • Milano – Gallarate – Varese
    • Milano Centrale – Arona – Domodossola
    • Milano – Luino
PUBLIC TRANSPORT ATM
The entrance to the concert area (WEST ACCESS TRIULZA) is near the RHO FIERA M1 stop (RED LINE) - TICKET ATM VALID FOR RHO FIERA IS NECESSARY. To facilitate the outflow we advise everyone to buy the round-trip ticket upon departure. Last departure from the terminus Rho Fieramilano at 00:10. To continue your journey late at night, you can use the night network buses (all lines on the website atm.it)

CAR
We recommend using Google Maps as a navigator, both on and off the show. From the A7-Genoa, A1-Bologna, A4-Turin highways: A50 motorway Ovest, direction Varese, exit FIERAMILANO. From the A4-Venice motorway: exit PERO-FIERAMILANO. From the A8-Varese, A9-Como motorways: from the Milan North barrier, A4-Venice direction and FIERAMILANO exit. If you were to accompany someone to the festival, we recommend using the address Via Achille Grandi, Rho (always via Google Maps). FROM MILAN Motorway A8 direction Varese-Como, exit FIERAMILANO. A4 motorway towards Turin, exit PERO-FIERAMILANO.
