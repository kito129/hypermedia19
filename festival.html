FESTIVAL

... Music ... Dance ... Theater. This is MILAN BY NIGHT. Get ready for a weekend full of great events just for you, in an exclusive location with unique emotions thanks to the tree of life that with its colors manages to create a spatial atmosphere around the great guests of the evenings!

MILAN BY NIGHT was born in 2012 from a group of 3 boys who wanted to organize a festival to entertain their friends with music, dance and theater performances.
In the early years the artists who performed on the "stage" were their acquaintances or however little-known guests, but quickly the festival gained fame and obtained ever more important partnerships until reaching today.
Simone, Andrea and Marco would never have expected to arrive where they are now even within 10 years, so that now this Festival is considered one of the most important at national level, with guests of the caliber of Vasco Rossi, just to name one.

PUBLIC TRANSPORT ATM
The entrance to the concert area (WEST ACCESS TRIULZA) is near the RHO FIERA M1 stop (RED LINE) - TICKET ATM VALID FOR RHO FIERA IS NECESSARY. To facilitate the outflow we advise everyone to buy the round-trip ticket upon departure. Last departure from the terminus Rho Fieramilano at 00:10. To continue your journey late at night, you can use the night network buses (all lines on the website atm.it)
